Directors

Stranger Things is a popular series created by Matt and Ross Duffer, twin brothers who are writers, directors, and producers. They were born on February 15, 1984, in Durham, North Carolina. Before Stranger Things, the brothers worked on the horror film Hidden 2015 and the series Wayward Pines. The show, which premiered in 2016 on Netflix, combines sci-fi, horror, and drama, inspired by films of the 1980s. The story takes place in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana. The Duffer brothers are known for their unique style, attention to detail, 80s atmosphere, and well-developed characters. They promise to make the final season of the series even more thrilling.
